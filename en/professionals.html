Frequently Asked Questions

Quick and Easy Answers to Common Questions.

01. Is the application free to use?
Professionals use the application completely free of charge!

You can use the application for free and you can apply to as many vacancies as you like.

02. Can I search other people's profiles?
No. Candidates cannot see each other in the application.

As a candidate, you have the opportunity to search for employers and open job positions. Only employers can see your profile on the app.

03. What will increase my chances of finding a job?
A detailed profile in the application.

Your chances of employment will increase if you have filled out your profile in detail. In this way, employers can see more information about you and make an employment decision more easily.

04. Is there a method by which candidates are ranked?
Professionals are sorted by geolocation.

The application ranks you based on your location entered in your profile. It does not have to be your residential address, but the desired address or the city where you are looking for a job.

05. Do I have the right to decline a job offer?
Of course.

If the job offer does not suit you, you can reject the same offer. Refusal of an offer does not affect future employment.

06. Is it necessary to fill in all the fields on the profile?
It is not mandatory, but it is recommended.

We advise you to fill out your profile in as much detail as possible, because that way you will attract more interest from a potential employer.

If you do not find the answer to your question listed within our FAQ's, you can always contact us directly by our chat!
