Frequently Asked Questions

Quick and Easy Answers to Common Questions about pricing.

01. Is it possible to cancel the subscription and how?
Subscription can be canceled.

All subscriptions are limited in time. You can cancel your subscription at any time and even before the end of the paid time period.

02. Can I switch from one plan to another if I change my mind?
Transition from one plan to another

At any time you can switch from one plan to another, from a more expensive to a cheaper one or vice versa. And you can cancel your subscription at any time.

03. What exactly does a subscription entail?
Subscription packages and discounts

In the price list, you can see that the packages differ in terms of time period, price and services. The biggest discount is available when purchasing an annual package (50%). The annual package includes the biggest discount on advertising (50%).

If you do not find the answer to your question listed within our FAQ's, you can always contact us directly by our chat!
